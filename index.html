About YUKTI-2K26

YUKTI is the flagship techno-cultural festival hosted at the Visvesvaraya Technological University Campus, Muddenahalli. The fest celebrates creativity, innovation, sportsmanship, and cultural expression among students from institutions across the region. Over the years, YUKTI has grown into a vibrant platform where young minds showcase their technical brilliance, artistic talents, and competitive spirit through a wide range of technical competitions, cultural performances, and sporting events. Hosted in the historic town of Muddenahalli — the birthplace of Sir M. Visvesvaraya, the festival reflects the legacy of engineering excellence and innovation. YUKTI continues to inspire students to push boundaries, explore new ideas, and celebrate the spirit of technology, culture, and teamwork.
